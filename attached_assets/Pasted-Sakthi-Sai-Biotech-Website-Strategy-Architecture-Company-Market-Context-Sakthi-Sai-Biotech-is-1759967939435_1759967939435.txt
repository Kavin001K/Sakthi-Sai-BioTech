Architecture & Navigation

The site structure should be simple, intuitive and optimized for lead capture. Key pages/sections include:

Homepage: Bold headline (e.g. “Advanced Agri-Chemicals Since 1999”), hero image (farm or product) and a brief value proposition. Include primary CTAs (“Request Quote”, “Product Catalog”) above the fold. Highlight three to four core offerings or benefits with icons or photos (e.g. Bactericides, Micronutrients, Growth Promoters).

Products Catalog: A navigable product section, organized by category (e.g. Fertilizers, Pesticides, Growth Promoters, Nutrient Supplements). Each product should have an image, description of use/benefits, and technical specs. Filters or a search function will help buyers find relevant products quickly. On each product page, include a prominent “Enquire Now” form or link.

About Us / Company: Detail Sakthi Sai’s history, mission and leadership. Mention Pollachi heritage and export markets to build credibility. (Pollachi’s agri-hub status
pollachifarmersmarket.com
 can be highlighted here.) Include any manufacturing certifications or awards.

Resources / Blog: A section for articles, news or whitepapers on agricultural topics (e.g. “Improving Soil Health”, “Crop Disease Management”). This establishes authority. Articles should be well-written and jargon-free; each article should have a clear call-to-action (e.g. “Download full brochure” or “Contact us for details”).

International / Exports: A dedicated page or section outlining export services (shipping, compliance, payment modes), and showcasing major markets (with flags or stats for Ethiopia, Indonesia etc.). Explain that the site supports multiple languages (with a language switcher visible in the header).

Contact Us: A simple page with contact form fields (name, company, email, inquiry), phone number, email, and maps. Place a quick-enquiry form in the footer of every page. Given the B2B context, also offer a “Request Callback” or WhatsApp contact for immediate responses.

Navigation: Use a clear top menu with broad categories (Products, About, Resources, Contact). If needed, use dropdowns for sub-categories. Follow conventional layouts (logo top-left, menu across top, search icon). Ensure mobile responsiveness (touch-friendly menus). Studies show users prefer simple, uncluttered interfaces

Since Sakthi Sai exports to Ethiopia and Indonesia, the website must support multiple languages. Provide content in English, Bahasa Indonesia and Amharic (or another major Ethiopian language) at minimum. A language selector (with flags or codes) should be prominent. High-quality translations demonstrate respect and build trust – studies show 65% of users are more likely to buy if information is in their native language

SEO & Marketing Integration

SEO Foundations: Use relevant keywords (e.g. “bio fungicide manufacturer India”, “micronutrient fertilizer supplier”) in titles, headings and meta tags. Optimize page load speed and mobile performance (Google rewards fast, mobile-friendly sites). Implement schema markup for products and company info if possible.

Lead Magnet: Offer downloadable resources (e.g. a PDF catalog or fertilizer guide) in exchange for email sign-ups. This both captures leads and provides value.

Analytics & Tracking: Integrate Google Analytics and Search Console to monitor traffic and behavior. Track form submissions to measure lead generation.

Content Marketing: Promote new blog posts on social media or B2B forums. Consider industry directories or Indiamart listings linking back to the site.
